# Solar Energy Sales Consultation Script

## Opening & Qualification

**Introduction:**
"Hi [Prospect Name], I'm [Your Name] from [Company Name]. I'm reaching out because homeowners in your area are saving up to 80% on their electricity bills by switching to solar. Do you have about 5 minutes to see how much you could be saving?"

**Key Qualification Questions:**
1. **Current Electricity Costs:** "What's your typical monthly electricity bill right now?"
2. **Home Ownership:** "Do you own your home, or are you renting?"
3. **Roof Age/Condition:** "How old is your current roof, and have you had any recent repairs?"
4. **Future Plans:** "Do you plan to stay in your home for the next 5-10 years?"
5. **Energy Usage:** "Do you work from home, or are there specific times when your energy usage is highest?"

## Financial Benefits Presentation

### Savings Breakdown
- **Immediate Savings:** 20-40% reduction in monthly electricity costs
- **Long-term Savings:** Up to 80% reduction over 25-year system lifespan
- **Fixed Energy Costs:** Lock in your energy rate for decades
- **Increased Home Value:** Average 4-6% increase in property value

### Example Calculation (Based on $200/month bill):
- Current 10-year cost: $24,000
- Solar 10-year cost: $4,800-9,600
- **Total Savings: $14,400-19,200**

## Government Incentives & Tax Credits

### Federal Incentives (2024):
- **30% Federal Tax Credit** on total system cost
- No income limits for most homeowners
- Can be combined with state/local incentives

### State/Local Programs:
- [State-specific rebates and programs]
- Net metering programs
- Property tax exemptions in many areas

## Common Objection Handling

### "Upfront Cost is Too High"
**Response:** "I understand that concern. That's why we offer $0-down financing options where you can start saving from day one. With the 30% federal tax credit and state incentives, most homeowners see a complete return on investment within 6-8 years."

### "My Roof Isn't Suitable"
**Response:** "Great question! We can assess your roof's suitability with satellite imaging and a quick inspection. Most homes are excellent candidates, and if your roof needs replacement, we can bundle that cost into the solar financing."

### "I'm Not Sure About ROI Timeline"
**Response:** "That's smart thinking. Based on your current $[monthly bill] electricity bill, you'd likely break even in [X] years. After that, you're getting free electricity for the remaining 15-20 years of the system's life. That's like getting a 20-year raise."

### "I Might Move Soon"
**Response:** "Actually, solar homes sell faster and for more money. Studies show solar panels increase home value by about 4-6%. If you move within the payoff period, you can either transfer the financing to the new owner or pay it off with your home sale profit."

### "What About Maintenance?"
**Response:** "Solar systems are incredibly reliable with virtually no maintenance required. They come with 25-year warranties, and most homeowners never need to touch them. Occasional cleaning is all that's needed."

## Closing & Call to Action

**Consultation Offer:**
"Based on what you've shared, I'm confident we can save you significant money on your energy bills. I'd like to schedule a free, no-obligation consultation where we can:

1. Analyze your specific energy usage and roof orientation
2. Provide a customized savings projection
3. Show you exact pricing and financing options
4. Answer any additional questions you have

The consultation takes about 30 minutes and can be done virtually or in person. Would Tuesday or Thursday afternoon work better for you?"

**Urgency Elements:**
- "The 30% federal tax credit is scheduled to decrease in [future year]"
- "Current utility rate increases are averaging 3-5% annually"
- "Installation timelines are currently [X] weeks due to high demand"
