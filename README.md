# Solar Energy Business Assistant

A smart tool for solar energy businesses to instantly generate 
AI-ready scripts tailored to your purpose, audience, and tone.

## What it does

Select your options and get a ready-to-use AI script for any 
solar business scenario:

- **Purpose** — Sales, Marketing, Customer Support, Content Creation, or Client Proposals
- **Audience** — Homeowners, Businesses, Both, or Government & Institutions
- **Tone** — Professional, Friendly, Urgent, or Educational

## How to use

1. Open the app
2. Choose your **Purpose**, **Audience**, and **Tone**
3. Click **Generate**
4. Copy the result and paste it into any AI tool

## Use cases

- Train an AI chatbot for your solar sales team
- Generate marketing copy for social media or ads
- Write client proposals faster
- Build a customer support knowledge base

## Tech stack

- HTML / CSS / JavaScript (vanilla)
- No frameworks, no dependencies
